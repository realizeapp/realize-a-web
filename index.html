THE SCIENCE

Realize is based on the theory of behavioral activation.

The idea behind behavioral activation is that by changing the context in which you live, you can change your mood. This involves engaging in activities that are meaningful and enhance well-being. This has been shown to be work, both in case studies (1, 2, 3, 4, 5) and larger scale studies.
